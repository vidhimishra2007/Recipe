Creamy White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients: Pasta, butter, flour, milk, cheese, salt, pepper, vegetables.

Procedure:

  1. Boil pasta until soft.
  2. Melt butter and add flour to prepare white sauce.
  3. Add milk slowly and stir continuously.
  4. Add vegetables and cooked pasta.
  5. Mix cheese and cook for 2-3 minutes.

Tip: Serve hot for best taste.

Veg Biryani

Ingredients: Rice, mixed vegetables, spices, onion, tomato, yogurt.

Procedure:

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. Prepare masala with onions and spices.
  3. Add vegetables and cook well.
  4. Layer rice and masala.
  5. Cook on low flame for 10 minutes.

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients: Flour, milk, eggs, sugar, baking powder, butter.

Procedure:

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add milk and eggs.
  3. Heat pan and pour batter.
  4. Cook both sides until golden brown.

Greek Salad

Ingredients: Cucumber, tomato, olives, feta cheese, olive oil.

Procedure:

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Add olives and cheese.
  3. Drizzle olive oil.
  4. Toss gently and serve fresh.
